Photography as a Second Language

Photography is my passion. I've been taking photos since I was very young with an analog Canon AV1 film camera. I currently shoot with a Canon DSLR 70D and the collection of different lenses including Canon wide 10-18mm F4/5.6, prime 50mm F1.8, prime wide 24mm F2.8, zoom 18-135mm F3.5/5.6, and zoom 75-300mm F4/5.6. I like street, low-light, minimalist, and B&W photography.
